REBUILDING A COMMUNITY

CNI believes that building sustainable and resilient livelihoods for people and families with vulnerabilities across south Sudan is the way to go. Empowerments of communities lead to greater participation and ownership by communities of their own development. Equipping individuals with information and basic skills to take responsibility for fixing their homesteads and restoring public facilities are the most effective ways to impact an entire community. So when you give to one of our projects, your contribution grows into more than just a repaired roof or a restored facility. You're helping rebuild entire communities of south Sudan.

REBUILDING PRIDE
